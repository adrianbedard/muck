The Mobile Maker Lab brings making and creativity to you! It can visit schools either as a single visit or a series of visits. A single visit field trip would consist of an overview and demonstration of the equipment available in the Mobile Maker Lab to introduce students to the tools. Students would learn about different manufacturing techniques along with the pros and cons for various applications. This single visit would give them a taste of what is out there in the way of new technologies in the maker community and the relationship to industry. The Mobile Maker Lab can also schedule a series of visits as part of an after school program. Students would get a more in depth experience with the equipment while they learn about designing in 2D and 3D, aeronautics, marine robotics, and more. They will be given activities, such as creating their own designs for the 3D printer or laser cutter, to work on between visits.
